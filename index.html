Biography

Houssam-Eddine Zahaf is a postdoctoral researcher in computer science at Lille University within the Embedded Real-Time Adaptative system Design and Execution (Émeraude) team of the CRIStAL Lab at Lille, France. His main research interests are in real-time systems, operating systems, scheduling algorithms, with a special focus on energy consumption and computing onto heterogeneous/unrelated platforms. Previously, he was a postdoctoral researcher/contractor at Nvidia Corporation in Hipert Lab, Unimore university in Italy. He was also a lecturer (ATER) at the Lille Computer Science Faculty (FIL).

He graduated from Oran University, Algeria in 2013 in Embedded Systems, within LAPECI LAB. In November 2016, he received a Ph.D degree in Computer Sciences from Lille university. Here is my detailed CV
